3-pot savings system

Akropolis Pension

An account designed for the long-term. Save into your voluntary pension with funds released when you reach age of retirement.

Akropolis Emergency Fund

Save for a rainy day or in case of emergency. Your funds will be made available to you when you need them the most.

Akropolis Short Term Savings

Short term savings are your best choice to save for vacations, vehicles, electronics, and increasing your quality of life. Save into this account where your funds will be ready to spend in the short term. You have full control over whether you'd like to spend or save.
